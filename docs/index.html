The Alison Project

A state-of-the-art sound recognition solution designed to help people suffering from hearing impairement all over the world.

Alison is an open source project developped with Python for Raspberry Pi. Calibrate the device to listen for some of the sounds of your house using our cross-platform mobile app. Alison will inform you of the occurence of these sounds in a visual way.

Calibration App

Train the Alison system to recognize the sounds you want using our cross-platform mobile application. The app requires access to the phone's microphone and bluetooth. No internet connection required.

Hardware specifications

As with our software, every piece of hardware we used and we developped follows our open-source philosophy.

Exterior hardware used

The core of our solution was designed and implemented on a Raspberry Pi 3B+ linked with a Respeaker vX.x. Information about the microphones and lighting solutions used if in the documentation
