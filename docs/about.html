The project

The Alison Project originates from a 4th Year research project at INSA Toulouse. It's an initiative of the GEI Department, and the outline was given to us by our tutors. You can find more information about the original specifications here (in french). The project started late January 2019, for an initial prototype release due May 24, 2019. We worked in sprints of about one month each, ending with a meeting with our tutors, setting new goals for the next sprint. There may be some new development following this initial project in late 2019, with the possible creation of a 5th Year ISS (Innovative Smart Systems) project.
